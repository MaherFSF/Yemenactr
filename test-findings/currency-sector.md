"Split Monetary System" - Yemen operates two separate monetary systems since 2019. New banknotes (printed after 2016) not accepted in Sana'a areas. Creates significant exchange rate gap.

## Multiple Sources Section:
Shows why exchange rates differ across sources:

3. **Reuters** (Composite rate): 1,665 YER/$

Explanation: CBY uses licensed office rates that may lag market. YETO survey captures street-level transactions. Reuters aggregates multiple dealer quotes.
