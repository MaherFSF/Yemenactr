## Data Quality and Gaps

- The primary data source was the Humanitarian Data Exchange (HDX). Several datasets were explored, but not all were successfully downloaded or contained data for the entire 2010-2026 period.
- The food prices and markets data from the World Food Programme failed to download due to a "NoSuchKey" error, indicating the files may no longer be available at the specified URLs.
- The conflict event datasets from ACLED were downloaded as XLSX files, but not processed in this phase. Further analysis is required to extract meaningful data from these files.
- The IPC food security data covers the period from 2018 to 2026, with projections for future years. Data for the years 2010-2017 is missing from this specific dataset.
- No specific data was found for GDP, growth rates, inflation, exchange rates, trade volumes, banking sector, or labor statistics from the explored datasets. This indicates a significant data gap in the current research.
