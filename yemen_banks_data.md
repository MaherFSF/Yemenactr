## Recent Developments (2024-2025)

1. **License Revocations (July 2024)**: CBY Aden revoked licenses of 6 banks for failing to relocate from Sana'a
2. **US Sanctions (April 2025)**: International Bank of Yemen (IBY) sanctioned by US Treasury for alleged Houthi support
3. **Bank Relocations (March 2025)**: 8 Yemeni banks applied to relocate headquarters to Aden amid international pressure
