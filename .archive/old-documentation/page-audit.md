### Confidence Rating System (A-D)
- A: ثقة عالية (High Confidence) - Official source, verified by multiple independent parties, recent data
- B: ثقة متوسطة (Medium Confidence) - Reliable source, limited verification, reasonably recent
- C: ثقة منخفضة (Low Confidence) - Single source, unverified, or old data requiring caution
- D: تقدير/بديل (Estimate/Proxy) - Calculated estimate, proxy indicator, or expert assessment
