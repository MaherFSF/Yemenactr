## Yemen-specific Findings and Projections

### Spring 2025 Report

* **Real GDP per capita:** Plummeted by 58% since 2015.
* **Poverty:** Affected 49% of the population before the conflict and has worsened significantly.
* **IRG Revenues (excluding grants):** Fell to 2.5% of GDP in 2024 from 4.6% in 2023.
* **Inflation (Aden):** Exceeded 30% by the end of 2024.
* **Exchange Rate (Aden):** Depreciated from YER 1,540/USD in January 2024 to YER 2,065/USD by year-end.


### Spring 2024 Report

* **Real GDP per capita:** Contracted by 54% between 2015 and 2023.
* **Poverty:** Most Yemenis are living in poverty.
* **Food Insecurity:** Half of the population (17.4 million) facing food insecurity.
* **Human Development Index:** Ranked 186 out of 191 countries in 2022.
* **Inflation (IRG areas):** Decelerated to 7.0% from 36.4% the previous year.
* **Inflation (Houthi-controlled areas):** Recorded a deflation of 11.8%.
* **Exchange Rate (Aden):** Depreciated to 1,532 YER per US$1 by the end of 2023.
