# Central Bank of Yemen - Aden Research Data

## Source: CBY Monthly Report - August 2024
URL: https://english.cby-ye.com/files/672c5ef5740a7.pdf

### Key Financial Data (August 2024)

**Money Supply:**
- Broad money supply (M2): YER 13,505.9 billion (end of August 2024)
- Increase of 1.1% (YER 149.6 billion) from July 2024

**Foreign Assets:**
- Central bank's foreign assets: YER 2,858.6 billion (end of August 2024)
- Compared to YER 2,766.7 billion at end of July 2024
- Increase of YER 91.9 billion or 3.3%

**Government Claims:**
- Net claims on government: YER 7,132.4 billion (August 2024)
- Decrease of YER 1.8 billion or 0.02% from July 2024

**Issued Currency:**
- Balance of issued currency: YER 3,670.3 billion

**Total Balance Sheet:**
- CBY total balance sheet: YER 11,594.6 billion (June 2024)
- Increase of YER 501.1 billion or 4.5% from previous period

### Additional Context from World Bank
- Foreign exchange reserves: ~US$1.52 billion (2023)
