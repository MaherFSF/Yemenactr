# UNDP Yemen Economy Publications Research



## Peace Support Facility for Yemen 2019 Annual Report

* **Publication Date:** July 6, 2020
* **URL:** https://www.undp.org/arab-states/publications/peace-support-facility-yemen-2019-annual-report
* **Key Findings:**
    * A comprehensive damage assessment of the Ports of Hodeidah, Salif and Ras Issa was undertaken, and priority rehabilitation interventions identified.
    * The works for the rehabilitation of the Training Center of Port Hodeidah have started and procurement for the remaining interventions is ongoing.
    * Hala Systems was contracted to ensure support to OSESGY to prepare for the monitoring of ceasefire initiatives.
    * PSF swiftly responded to OSESGY request for support on port revenue management.
* **Economic Indicators:** Port revenue management.


## Independent Country Programme Evaluation: Yemen

* **Publication Date:** April 14, 2025
* **URL:** https://www.undp.org/evaluation/publications/icpe-yemen
* **Key Findings:** This Independent Country Programme Evaluation (ICPE) covers UNDP programme activities from 2019 to 2023, in the areas of livelihood and economic recovery, climate change, energy, water and environment, governance and rule of law, and peace support processes. It provides a set of forward-looking recommendations, with a view to supporting the country office and its partners in the continuous efforts to improve the country programme by learning from the achievements and challenges.
* **Economic Indicators:** Livelihood and economic recovery.

## Measuring Multidimensional Poverty in Yemen

* **Publication Date:** February 26, 2024
* **URL:** https://www.undp.org/arab-states/publications/measuring-multidimensional-poverty-yemen
* **Key Findings:** This report is Yemen´s first on the calculation of the sub-national Multidimensional Poverty Index (MPI) based on the 2021 Yemen Human Development Survey. It covers the sub-national level (representative regions), urban and rural areas of Al Bayda, Ta’iz, Hadramawt, Shabwah, Aden, Lahj, Ma’rib, Al Maharah and Ad Dali’ governorates. Results indicate that the percentage of people living in multidimensional poverty in Yemen was 82.7%.
* **Economic Indicators:** Multidimensional Poverty Index (MPI).""


## HUMAN DEVELOPMENT REPORT 2023-2024

* **Publication Date:** April 11, 2024
* **URL:** https://yemen.un.org/en/265715-human-development-report-2023-2024
* **Key Findings:** The 2023/24 Human Development Report assesses the dangerous gridlock resulting from uneven development progress, intensifying inequality, and escalating political polarization. The report emphasizes how global interdependence is being reconfigured and proposes a path forward where multilateralism plays a pivotal role.
* **Economic Indicators:** Human Development Index (HDI), inequality, political polarization.
