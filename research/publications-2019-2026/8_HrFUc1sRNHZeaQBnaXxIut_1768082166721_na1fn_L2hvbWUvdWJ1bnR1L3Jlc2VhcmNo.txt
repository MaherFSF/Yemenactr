Organization: Central Bank of Yemen - Aden


Publication:
Title: Annual Report 2020
Date: 2020
URL: https://english.cby-ye.com/files/634f8f90b9de5.pdf

Key Findings:
- Real GDP contracted by 8.5% in 2020.
- Annual inflation rate rose to 35% at the end of 2020.
- Money supply (M2) increased by 15% in 2020.
- The US dollar exchange rate against the Yemeni riyal reached 669 riyals per dollar at the end of 2020.
- The overall balance of the state's general budget in 2020 showed a cash deficit of 782 billion riyals.

- Public Domestic Debt
- Balance of Payments
- Public External Debt

Publication:
Title: Monetary and Financial Developments September 2024
Date: September 2024
URL: https://english.cby-ye.com/files/6735a2223f682.pdf

Key Findings:
- Broad money supply (M2) increased by 0.3%.
- The average exchange rate of the Yemeni rial against the US dollar in the parallel market was 1,905.40 YER/USD.
- The overall budget deficit was YER 262.9 billion.
- Domestic public debt increased by 0.1%.
- The consumer price index (CPI) recorded a value of 101.07, indicating a decrease in the monthly inflation rate to 1.45%.
