## Bank Relocation Status (2025)

Following the US designation of Houthis as a Foreign Terrorist Organization (FTO) in January 2025, several banks relocated their headquarters from Sana'a to Aden:

### Banks That Relocated to Aden (March-July 2025)
1. Tadhamon Bank
2. Al-Kuraimi Islamic Microfinance Bank
3. Shamil Bank of Yemen & Bahrain
4. Islamic Bank of Yemen for Finance and Investment
5. Yemen Bahrain Bank
6. Yemen Gulf Bank
7. Saba Islamic Bank
8. Yemeni Bank for Reconstruction and Development

### CBY Aden Announcement (July 31, 2025)
Governor Ahmed Ghaleb confirmed the completion of relocating the country's banking system to Aden.
