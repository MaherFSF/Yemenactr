# Yemen LNG Research Data

## Source: Wikipedia - Yemen LNG
URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yemen_LNG

### Company Overview
- **Name:** Yemen LNG (YLNG) - الشركة اليمنية للغاز الطبيعي المسال
- **Industry:** LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas)
- **Founded:** 1995
- **Headquarters:** Balhaf, Hadhramout, Yemen
- **Website:** yemenlng.com

### Production Capacity
- **Total capacity:** 6.7 million tonnes per year (mtpa)
- **Configuration:** Two LNG trains
- **Pipeline:** 320 km, 38-inch pipeline from Marib-Jawf field to Balhaf

### Project Cost
- **Total investment:** US$4 billion
- **Debt financing:** US$3 billion from bank syndicate

### Operational Status
- **Production started:** October 15, 2009
- **Operations halted:** April 2015 (due to Yemeni Civil War)
- **Current status:** Suspended since 2015

### Key Dates
- 1995: Company incorporated
- 1997: Development halted (Asian economic crisis)
- 2005: Project relaunched (August)
- 2007: 20-year gas supply contract with Safer
- 2009: First production
- 2015: Operations suspended
- 2019: Latest pipeline attack (June)
