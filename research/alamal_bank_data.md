# Al-Amal Microfinance Bank Research Data

## Source: Official Website (alamalbank.com)
URL: https://alamalbank.com/en/

### Company Overview
- **Name:** Al-Amal Microfinance Bank (AMB) - بنك الأمل للتمويل الأصغر
- **Established:** 2002 (under Yemeni Law No. 23 of 2002)
- **Type:** Non-dividend financial institution (non-profit)
- **Regulator:** Central Bank of Yemen
- **Distinction:** First Microfinance bank in Yemen and MENA region

### Key Statistics (from website)
- **Years of operation:** 15+ years
- **Branches:** 18 branches
- **Local & International Partners:** 15
- **Savings Accounts:** 327,838
- **Number of Accounts:** 130,189
- **Loans Distributed:** 18,875
- **Cash Transfers:** 1,325,056
- **Partner Organizations:** 30

### Customer Demographics
- Male clients: 88%
- Female clients: 11%

### Services Offered
1. **Micro financing Products** - Financing for small and micro-enterprises
2. **Non-financial Services** - Training and rehabilitation
3. **Deposits and Savings** - Sharia-compliant bank accounts
4. **Remittances** - Domestic and international bank transfers
5. **Social Transfers** - Cash transfer services
6. **Electronic Money** - PYES mobile app for financial management

### Target Groups
- Impoverished groups in Yemen
- Low and limited income people
- SME entrepreneurs
