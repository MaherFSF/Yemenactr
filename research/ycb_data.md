# Yemen Commercial Bank Research Data

## Source: Official Website (ycb.bank)
URL: https://ycb.bank/en/about-bank

### Company Overview
- **Name:** Yemen Commercial Bank (YCB) - البنك التجاري اليمني
- **Type:** Yemeni closed joint-stock company
- **Established:** January 28, 1993
- **Legal Basis:** Central Bank of Yemen Decision No. 2783
- **Commercial License:** Ministry of Supply & Commerce Decision No. 32 of 1993
- **Distinction:** First Yemeni private commercial bank established after Yemen unification

### Key Statistics
- **ATMs:** 70+ operating ATMs across Yemen
- **Awards:** 6 consecutive years winner - The Banker Award (British agency)
- **Employee Count:** 201-500 employees (from ZoomInfo data)

### Vision
To be the pioneering and leading bank in the banking industry.

### Mission
To provide inclusive and advanced banking services to fulfill all needs of customers, exceeding their expectations.

### Core Values
- Transparent processes and transactions
- Continuous improvement to satisfy customers

### Services Offered
1. **Traditional Banking Services** - Individual and corporate accounts
2. **Digital Banking:**

   - ATM services

   - Phone Banking

3. **Money Transfers:**
   - Western Union Money Transfers

4. **Cards:**
   - Tejari Card
5. **E-Wallets:**

6. **Commercial Services:**
