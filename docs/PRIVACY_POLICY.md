## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use YETO services, we collect:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, organization affiliation (optional)
- **Profile Data**: User preferences, language choice (Arabic/English), sector interests
- **Saved Content**: Watchlists, saved searches, bookmarked indicators
- **Communications**: Messages sent to our support team or feedback forms

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

When you access YETO, we automatically collect:

- **Usage Data**: Pages viewed, features accessed, time spent on platform
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address (anonymized)
- **Log Data**: Access times, error logs, performance metrics
- **Analytics**: Aggregated usage patterns (via privacy-respecting analytics)

### 3. Information from Third Parties

- **Authentication**: Via Manus OAuth (only basic profile information)
- **Payment Processors**: For subscription management (we do not store payment card details)

### 4. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Location Data**: We do not track your geographic location
- **Biometric Data**: No facial recognition or fingerprint data
- **Social Media Activity**: We do not track your activity on other platforms
- **Sensitive Personal Data**: No health, religious, or political affiliation data

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses

1. **Service Delivery**
   - Provide access to YETO platform and features
   - Personalize your experience (saved preferences, watchlists)
   - Send platform updates and notifications you've subscribed to

2. **Platform Improvement**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve functionality
   - Identify and fix technical issues
   - Develop new features based on user needs

3. **Communication**
   - Respond to support requests
   - Send important service announcements
   - Share optional newsletter and research updates (with consent)

4. **Security and Compliance**
   - Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
   - Enforce our Terms of Service
   - Comply with legal obligations

### Data Minimization Principle

We only collect information necessary for these purposes. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location

- **Primary Database**: Encrypted MySQL database hosted on secure cloud infrastructure
- **Backup Storage**: Encrypted backups stored in geographically distributed locations
- **File Storage**: S3-compatible storage with encryption at rest and in transit

### Security Measures

1. **Encryption**
   - TLS/SSL for all data in transit
   - AES-256 encryption for data at rest
   - Encrypted database connections

2. **Access Controls**
   - Role-based access control (RBAC)
   - Multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
   - Regular access audits

3. **Infrastructure Security**
   - Regular security patches and updates
   - Intrusion detection systems
   - DDoS protection
   - Regular security audits

4. **Data Isolation**
   - User data segregated from public economic data
   - Personal information isolated from analytics

### Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Rationale |
|-----------|------------------|-----------|
| Account Information | Active account lifetime + 30 days | Service provision |
| Usage Logs | 90 days | Security and debugging |
| Analytics (aggregated) | 2 years | Platform improvement |
| Support Communications | 3 years | Legal compliance |
| Deleted Account Data | 30 days (backup retention) | Recovery window |

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Share Data Only In These Circumstances:

1. **Service Providers**
   - Hosting infrastructure (AWS, TiDB Cloud)
   - Authentication services (Manus OAuth)
   - Analytics (privacy-respecting, anonymized)
   - Email delivery (if enabled)
   
   **Safeguards**: All service providers bound by data processing agreements

2. **Legal Requirements**
   - Court orders or legal processes
   - Government requests where legally obligated
   - Protection of rights, property, or safety
   
   **Transparency**: We will notify users unless prohibited by law

3. **Business Transfers**
   - If YETO is acquired or merged, user data may transfer to new entity
   - Users will be notified and given option to delete accounts

### We NEVER:
- Sell your personal information
- Share data for third-party marketing
- Disclose research activity without explicit consent
- Provide user lists to any organization

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### Cookies We Use

1. **Essential Cookies** (Required)
   - Session management
   - Authentication state
   - Language preference
   - CSRF protection

2. **Analytics Cookies** (Optional)
   - Aggregated usage statistics
   - Performance monitoring
   - Feature usage tracking
   
   **Privacy**: We use privacy-respecting analytics (Plausible/Umami) that do not track individuals

3. **Preference Cookies** (Optional)
   - Theme selection (light/dark mode)
   - Dashboard layout preferences
   - Saved sector filters

### Cookie Control

You can control cookies through:
- Browser settings (block all or specific cookies)
- Platform settings (analytics opt-out)
- Cookie consent banner (granular control)

**Note**: Disabling essential cookies may limit platform functionality.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Subject Rights (GDPR/CCPA Compliant)

1. **Right to Access**
   - Request a copy of your personal data
   - View data processing activities
   - Export your data in machine-readable format

2. **Right to Rectification**
   - Correct inaccurate information
   - Update outdated information
   - Complete incomplete data

3. **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**
   - Delete your account and associated data
   - Remove specific data items
   - Exception: Data required for legal compliance

4. **Right to Restrict Processing**
   - Limit how we use your data
   - Object to specific processing activities
   - Withdraw consent for optional features

5. **Right to Data Portability**
   - Export your data in JSON or CSV format
   - Transfer data to another platform

6. **Right to Object**
   - Opt out of analytics tracking
   - Unsubscribe from optional communications
   - Disable personalization features

### How to Exercise Your Rights

- **Account Settings**: Many controls available in user settings
- **Email Request**: privacy@yeto.org
- **Data Export Tool**: Available in account dashboard
- **Response Time**: We respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA)

## Children's Privacy

YETO is not directed at children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we discover that a child under 16 has provided personal information, we will:
1. Delete the information immediately
2. Terminate the account
3. Notify the parent/guardian if contact information is available

Parents who believe their child has provided information should contact: privacy@yeto.org

## Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that compromises personal information:

1. **Internal Response** (within 24 hours)
   - Contain and assess breach
   - Preserve evidence
   - Begin remediation

2. **User Notification** (within 72 hours)
   - Email notification to affected users
   - Platform announcement
   - Details on data compromised and mitigation steps

3. **Regulatory Notification** (as required by law)
   - GDPR: Within 72 hours to supervisory authority
   - Other jurisdictions: Per local requirements

## Privacy by Design

YETO is built with privacy as a core principle:

1. **Minimal Data Collection**: Only collect what we need
2. **Purpose Limitation**: Use data only for stated purposes
3. **Transparency**: Clear explanations of data practices
4. **User Control**: Granular privacy settings
5. **Security First**: Encryption and access controls by default
6. **Anonymous by Default**: Public economic data not linked to user accounts
7. **No Dark Patterns**: No tricks to get you to share more data
